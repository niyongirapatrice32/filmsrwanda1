Welcome to Rwanda Tea & Tours

Experience the rich flavors of Rwandan tea, grown in the lush hills of Gisovu and Gisakura estates. Pair your purchase with an unforgettable journey through Rwanda’s stunning landscapes, from Nyungwe National Park to gorilla trekking in Volcanoes National Park.

Premium Rwandan Tea

Our teas are sourced from Rwanda’s finest estates, known for their fertile volcanic soil and ideal climate. Enjoy award-winning teas from Gisovu Tea Estate, certified by the Rainforest Alliance, and the scenic Gisakura Tea Estate.

Explore Rwanda’s Wonders

Rwanda, the Land of a Thousand Hills, offers breathtaking scenery and unique experiences. Book a tour with us to visit iconic destinations.

Top Attractions

  • Nyungwe National Park

    Explore the primeval rainforest, home to Colobus monkeys and the source of the Nile River. Join our guided tea estate tours at Gisovu and Gisakura, located near Nyungwe.

  • Volcanoes National Park

    Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime gorilla trekking adventure to see Rwanda’s majestic mountain gorillas.

  • Kigali City Tour

    Discover Rwanda’s vibrant capital, including the Kigali Genocide Memorial and local markets.
